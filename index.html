Église Saint Maurice

The Église Saint-Maurice is a beautiful old church in the historic centre of Lille, northern France. It has many lovely pieces of art and pretty stained glass, which makes this church simply charming. Its construction began at the end of the 14th century and completed at the end of the 19th century, and it was extended over more than four centuries. Built on top of an old place dedicated to Mars, the roman God of War, this church has an unique orientation and is the only one to be dedicated to Saint Maurice in the North of the France.

Address: 19 Parvis Saint-Maurice, 59800 Lille.
